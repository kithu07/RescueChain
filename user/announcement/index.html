    Urgent Updates from Wayanad Relief Camp

    Dear Volunteers and Donors,

    We are experiencing an unprecedented situation in the Wayanad relief camp due to severe flooding. Your prompt assistance and support are crucial. Here are the latest updates:

    • Current Camp Population: The relief camp is currently housing approximately 1,500 inhabitants, including families and individuals displaced by the flood.
    • Immediate Needs:
      • Medical Supplies: We urgently require first-aid kits, bandages, antiseptics, and antibiotics to manage the increasing number of injuries and illnesses.
      • Food and Water: There is an immediate need for non-perishable food items and clean drinking water to sustain the camp residents.
    • Volunteer Alert:
      • Medical Volunteers: We need certified medical professionals to assist with emergency care and treatment.
      • Logistics Volunteers: Support is needed for managing the distribution of supplies and organizing shelter arrangements.
    • Emergency Contact: For urgent queries or to offer direct assistance, please contact our emergency helpline at +91-9876543210.
    • New Developments:
      • Shelter Expansion: We are expanding the temporary shelters to accommodate the increasing number of displaced individuals.
      • Community Assistance Program: We are setting up a community assistance program to provide psychological support and counseling to affected families.

    We deeply appreciate your support during this critical time. Your donations and volunteer efforts are making a significant difference. For more information or to contribute, please use the options provided on our website.

    Thank you for your continued support.
